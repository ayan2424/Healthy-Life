Poses by Type

Cardio Blast

High-intensity moves to elevate your heart rate and improve cardiovascular health.

Do's:
  • ✓ Maintain a steady pace.
  • ✓ Keep your core engaged.
Don'ts:
  • ✗ Don't hold your breath.
  • ✗ Avoid locking your knees.

Strength & Sculpt

Poses designed to build muscle, increase bone density, and improve overall strength.

Do's:
  • ✓ Focus on proper form.
  • ✓ Use controlled movements.
Don'ts:
  • ✗ Don't use momentum to lift.
  • ✗ Avoid arching your back.

Flexibility Flow

Stretches and movements to increase your range of motion and reduce muscle tension.

Do's:
  • ✓ Breathe deeply into stretches.
  • ✓ Move slowly and gently.
Don'ts:
  • ✗ Don't bounce in stretches.
  • ✗ Avoid pushing past your limit.

Benefits of Daily Practice

Meditation is a practice where an individual uses a technique – such as mindfulness, or focusing the mind on a particular object, thought, or activity – to train attention and awareness, and achieve a mentally clear and emotionally calm and stable state.

  • Reduces Stress & Anxiety
  • Enhances Self-Awareness
  • Improves Concentration & Memory
  • Promotes Emotional Health
